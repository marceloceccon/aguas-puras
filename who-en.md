### Who is calling for this

I’m Marcelo Ceccon — the guy who spent his teenage years on a 13-meter sailboat while my parents, Marçal and Eneida, took us around the world on Rapunzel. I’ve tasted clean ocean water at dawn in the middle of the Pacific and watched it turn brown with runoff in half the ports we hit. Thirty years later I’m still the same person who can’t shut up about water, except now I ship code instead of sails. 

I want to built the idea for AguasPuras in Jurerê because I’m tired of watching Florianópolis — and every other coastal city — pretend the data is fine while the beaches close and the tourists quietly stop coming back. 

This isn’t a whitepaper. It’s the project I dream about while doing what I love, being in the water.

### Who can actually make this happen

I’ve spent the last three decades shipping startups, fintech systems, and now safe AI multi-agent tools. I know how to move fast on-chain, how to make AI actually useful instead of decorative, and how to turn raw data into decisions that don’t get ignored. 

If you’re a builder, a scientist, a founder, or someone who simply refuses to accept polluted water as normal, come help.
